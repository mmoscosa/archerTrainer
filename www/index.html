About

About bowTrainer

Introduction

bowTrainer was created by mmoscosa who is an Archer. He began his shooting experience in Mexico and from where then he movd to Scotland and kept on his practicing.

mmoscosa realized two things:

  1. The need to keep track of his records was a must when training.
  2. Current archery apps dont really integrate technology with traditional ways of score records
    • There are many great apps out there but archers tend to stop using them and then ditching them for pencil and paper.

This apps then aims to achieve the already stated and adding some other features than are useful for every archer. Plus integrating social networking (yes yet another social network) to keep the archer comunity together; allowing them to train together, even if they live in different parts of the world.

Sections

Add

Here the user will be able to add a new shooting round. He or she will be able to choose between the following categories:

Fita
  • Indoor
    • 18mts
  • Outdoor
    • 30mts
    • 50mts
    • 70mts
    • 90mts
GNAS
  • Indoor
    • 20yds
  • Outdoor
    • 40yds
    • 60yds
    • 80yds
    • 100yds

He or she will be able to choose if it is a practice or a tournament round; this to keep records straight. Aditionally every round will concist of 6 arrows.

Equipment

Here you will be able to add your bow and bowsights.

Record

You will see all your previous shooting rounds divided by categories that already states on the Add section.

Profile

You will see your contact details, and your fellow archers.

App

The app was created using jQuery mobile and exporting it using Phonegap to ensure corss platform with possibility to access the app as a webapp through any web browser.

Notice

mmoscosa is aware that he might lack of many FITA or GNAS rules and more many other things. If you spot the lack of or mistake in any of the features in this app version, use the Contact function to drop me a line and add this features in following versions.
